Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my data across devices?

Make sure you're signed into iCloud on all devices. Your data syncs automatically.

How do I connect to Apple Health?

Go to Settings → Apple Health → Enable "Sync Workouts". You'll be prompted to grant permissions.

Can I switch between Full Gym and Home Gym?

Yes! Go to Settings → Program → Variant to switch anytime.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings → Reset Program. You can also delete the app to remove all local data.
