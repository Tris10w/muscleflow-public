Privacy Policy

MuscleFlow - Your Privacy Matters

Information We Collect

MuscleFlow collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Workout data including exercises, sets, reps, and weights
  • Your app preferences and settings
  • Reminder schedules
  • Workout history and progress metrics

This data is stored exclusively on your device and, if enabled, synced to your personal iCloud account.

iCloud Sync

When iCloud sync is enabled, your workout data is stored in your private iCloud container. This data:

  • Is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Is only accessible by you through your Apple ID
  • Syncs across your devices signed into the same Apple ID
  • Can be deleted by removing the app or clearing iCloud data

We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Apple Health Integration

If you enable Apple Health integration, MuscleFlow may:

  • Read your body weight to track fitness progress
  • Write completed workouts to Apple Health, including duration, calories burned, and workout type

Health data permissions can be managed in the iOS Settings app under Privacy & Security > Health > MuscleFlow.

Data Sharing

MuscleFlow does not:

  • Sell your personal data
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Collect any data beyond what is necessary for app functionality

Your fitness data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as you use the app. You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Using the "Reset Program" option in Settings
  • Deleting the app from your device
  • Removing MuscleFlow data from iCloud Settings

Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

Children's Privacy

MuscleFlow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
